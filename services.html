    Authentication of deeds

    We have competence to authenticate private deeds legally made between individuals, companies and other organizations whenever they need to file or use authentic deeds. Deeds that can be authenticated include but not limited to minutes of the meetings of organizations, resolutions of Board of directors of companies, contracts, and many more others.

    View More

    Authentication of signatures

    We have the power to authenticate different signatures depending on drawers and anticipated use. Professionalism and compliance with laws is assured at all levels.

    View More

    Certification of document authenticity

    Some documents are authentic but that authenticity is not known to all people except the drawer and the holder. By request of interested parties and after verification with all legal means our notaries may certify authenticity of a document before being used.

    View More

    Certification of the conformity

    More often, useful documents like degrees, transcripts, certificates, extracts are often issued in a single copy which the owner or beneficiary needs to hold all the time. To be assured of the conformity, some people request notarized copies and we have the power to certify with stamps conformity or a copy to the original after verification and assurance of that conformity.

    View More

    Issuance of copies of deeds drawn

    Some deeds are drawn by and before the Notary who keeps the original copy of the deed. If requested by the beneficiaries or qualified people, the Notary issues copies of all copies they drew up or drawn before them as provided by the law.

    View More

    Certification of wills

    We have the power to certify different types of wills and authenticate them following the provisions of the law governing succession and will in particular. The wills subject to certification include those involving movable, immovable and other types of properties that can be given by the owner. We also authenticate or certify wills relating to assignment of rights and liabilities.

    View More

    Certification and authentication of complex deeds

    Some deeds are complex in their nature following the number of parties involved, the value of money in question, the number of transactions to be concluded or other factors that may not assure their sincerity in the future. We have the ability and competence to verify and certify or authenticate complex deeds.
